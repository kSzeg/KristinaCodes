Kristina's Magical Balls

Delicious chocolatey balls you don't have to bake!

What you'll be needin!

  • One package of Oreos
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 8 ounces of baker's white chocolate
  • Optional: Food coloring to dye your balls

What you'll be doin!

  1. Crush up oreos anyway you see fit. It should be about the consistency of playground sand.
      2. ***Don't worry if there are small chunks
  2. Put crushed oreos into a bowl, and using your hands mix in cream cheese.
      3. ***Make sure to blend the ingredients completely!
  3. Place the mix into the fridge for about 30-45 minutes
  4. Remove mix from the fridge and roll into balls. I usually say a little smaller than a golf ball.
  5. Melt the baker's chocolate following the directions on the package.
  6. Use tooth picks to dip your balls in the melted white chocolate.
      7. ***If you like you can drizzle milk or dark chocolate on your balls.
  7. Place your balls on a baking sheet covered in wax paper so they can cool down.
      8. ***For faster cooling, place them in the fridge.
  8. Wait 60 pain staking minutes.
  9. Take your balls out of the fridge and serve.

ENJOY MY BALLS!
